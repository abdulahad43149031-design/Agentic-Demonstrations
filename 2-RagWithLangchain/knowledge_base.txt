# AcmeCloud Customer Support Knowledge Base

## Billing

- Customers can request a refund within 30 days of purchase.
- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.
- Monthly subscriptions can be cancelled anytime from the Account Settings page
- Annual subscriptions are non-refundable after 30 days.

## Password Reset

- Click "Forgot Password" on the login page.
- A password reset link is sent to the registered email.
- The link expires after 15 minutes.

## API

- The base API URL is https://api.acmecloud.com/v1
- Authentication uses Bearer Tokens.
- Rate limit is 100 requests per minute.

## Technical Support

- Restarting the application resolves many common issues.
- Clear the application cache if the dashboard fails to load.
- Contact support if the issue persists after troubleshooting.

## Contact

- Support Email: support@acmecloud.com
- Business Hours: Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM UTC.
